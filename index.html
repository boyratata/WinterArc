Winter Arc

Step 1: Visualization

  • Visualize what would you be after it.
  • Keep the image of success clear in your mind.

Step 2: Write Down Your Goals

  • Set clear, achievable goals.
  • Review and update them regularly.

Step 3: Daily Habits

  • Workout 30+ minutes
  • Meditation 5+ minutes
  • Visualization 5+ minutes
  • Bible Reading: 5+ pages
  • Journal
  • Pray
  • Work on Purpose

Step 4: No-Go List

  • No Drugs
  • No Junk Food
  • No Social Media
  • No Fap
  • No Entertainment

Step 5: Fasting & Sabbath Day

  • Observe weekly fasting.
  • Dedicate a Sabbath day for rest.

Step 6: Accountability Partner

Find a partner who will help keep you accountable.

Step 7: Create Habit Lists

Develop checklists for your daily, weekly, and monthly habits.
